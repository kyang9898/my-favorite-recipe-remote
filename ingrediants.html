Ingrediants

  1. 1 lb of ground beef
  2. 1 tablespoon of oil
  3. 1 tablespoon of lemongrass paste
  4. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon of sugar
  6. 1-2 red chilli peppers (Adjust accordingly to your spice level, can me thinly sliced or minced)
  7. 3 tablespoons of lime juice
  8. 3 tablespoons of fish sauce
  9. 1-2 shallots, thinly sliced
  10. Fresh mint, roughly chopped
  11. Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
  12. Fresh Thai basil, roughly chopped

How to Serve

  • Get plate
  • Prepare fresh lettuce leaves
  • Cooked jasmine rice or sticky rice
  • Lime wedges
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Fresh mint
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Fresh Thai basil
