How to Make: Thai Beef Larb

  1. In a large frying pan or wok, heat oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add lemongrass paste, garlic, sugar and chilis and cook for 2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef and cook for 5 minutes or until fully cooked and no pink remains. Stir frequently while cooking to break the beef into small pieces.
  3. Add lime juice and fish sauce to the pan and stir until combined. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and mix in red onion, green onions, mint, cilantro, and Thai basil.
